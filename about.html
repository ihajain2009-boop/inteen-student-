About InTeen

Our Mission

InTeen aims to create meaningful opportunities for high school students by connecting them with learning-focused internships where they can grow, experiment, and find real-world exposure.

What We Do

• Connecting Students & Startups

We help high school students find internships designed for learning, not pressure.

• Providing Skill-Based Opportunities

Students get to explore roles ranging from content creation to design, research, and more.

• A Safe, Guided Space

We offer a friendly chat space where interns can communicate, ask questions, and support one another.

Note From the Founders

As high school students ourselves, we know the excitement and confusion that comes with trying to build your future. InTeen was created with that feeling in mind — a place where you don’t have to have everything figured out, but you can start somewhere meaningful.
